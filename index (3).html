While they nudged me towards a less sociable path, I discovered a peculiar kinship with numbers, finding solace in the intricate world of data analysis.

Realizing the importance of balancing my affinity for numbers with necessary social skills, I decided to plunge into the unfamiliar. For five years, I volunteered at a special recreational program for individuals with special needs. It was a tough initiation, having never interacted with mentally challenged individuals before.
